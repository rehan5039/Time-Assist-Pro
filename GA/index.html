User Manual - Gmail Alias Master

What is Gmail Alias Generator?

Gmail Alias Generator is a tool that helps you create multiple email aliases from your Gmail address. These aliases can be used to organize your inbox, sign up for services, or maintain privacy.

How to Use:

  1. Enter your Gmail address in the input field
  2. Click the "Generate Aliases" button
  3. View your generated aliases in the results section
  4. Use the copy button to copy individual aliases
  5. Use "Copy All" to copy all aliases at once

Benefits:

  • Organize emails by using different aliases for different purposes
  • Protect your privacy by using aliases for online signups
  • Track which services are sharing your email address
  • Filter and manage emails based on which alias they were sent to

Features:

  • Dark/Light mode toggle for comfortable viewing
  • Compact view option for better space utilization
  • Multiple color schemes to personalize your experience
  • Copy functionality for easy alias usage

Important Notes:

  • All aliases will deliver emails to your main Gmail inbox
  • Aliases are created using the Gmail plus addressing feature
  • Some websites might not accept plus addresses
